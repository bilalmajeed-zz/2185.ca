Purpose


Our mission is to inspire and develop students of Etobicoke Collegiate Institute to become experienced in the engineering field. Through science, technology and teamwork we can acheive this. The goal in becoming the top robotic influence school in Canada keeps us motivated in achieving greatness. Our main mission as a team is to spread the FIRST message.

Hierarchy


At the top of our hiearchy is our Project Manager, which is also our team captain, followed by 6 sub-team captains. Each captain is in charge of following: Communications, Drive Systems and Controls, Programming, Electrical, Design, and Machining. To avoid disorganization, the team leader meets with all the sub-team captains, every week to discuss our goals, deadlines and other concerns
